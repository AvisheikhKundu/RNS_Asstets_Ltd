Frequently Asked Questions

What is the down payment requirement?

We offer flexible payment plans starting with as low as 10-20% down payment. The exact percentage depends on the property and project type. Contact our sales team for customized payment options.

How long is the typical delivery timeline?

Most projects are completed within 2-3 years from launch. We maintain a strict schedule and provide monthly updates to investors. Some premium properties are move-in ready within 6-12 months.

Are your properties legally verified?

Yes, absolutely. All our properties undergo rigorous legal verification. We provide complete documentation including land title, architectural approvals, and utility connections.

What locations do you operate in?

We primarily focus on premium locations in Dhaka including Gulshan, Baridhara, Banani, Dhanmondi, and Mirpur. We also have projects in Chattogram and Sylhet.

Do you offer property management services?

Yes, we provide comprehensive property management services for investors. Our team handles maintenance, tenant management, and rent collection.

Can I get a site visit?

Absolutely! We offer regular site visits and virtual tours. Visit our contact page or call us to schedule a visit at your convenience.
